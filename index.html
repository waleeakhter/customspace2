Made Local

Through our decentralised network of manufacturers,
your custom-made pieces are made by certified
local makers. The digital designs on our platform are
encrypted and streamed to the machines of the
nearest maker reducing the distances needed to
transport your carefully made pieces.

No more dealing with anonymous call centers - you
can talk to a real person about your order, with the
knowledge that they are intimitely connected with
the piece that they are making.

At Custom Space we hope to foster local jobs and
businesses that were previously shut out due to the
economies of scale that the big brands monopolise.
